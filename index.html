How I Got Started

People imagine journalists at their typewriters with their top-hats. I've never subscribed to that idea. And that began was the start of My2Cents Radio.

As a fan and creator of public radio, I never understood their pledge drives. We will stop giving you content you can get on the internet on demand. So we can capture your attention enough for you to give us money ... over the phone.

So I created My2Cents Radio with a multi-disciplinary team to target listeners like me using an app. That listener could give while they listened. Click here to watch the pitch.

What's Next?
